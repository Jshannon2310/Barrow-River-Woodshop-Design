About Us :

Barrow River Woodshop Design is a family run business. We have a passion and love for what we do and only use the best equiptment. With the help of our StoneyCNC I2-R CNC machine we can personilse and really make your product special.

As you browse through our product selection you will find a fantastic collection of premium quality products. At Barrow River Woodshop Design we seek out some of the finest Irish harwoods, Character and Grain are at the core of the decision making in the selection process. We have carefully selected each of our products for their unique design and look. Every product in our site is a true reflection of the labours and passions of the individual makers. We aim to be your Chopping and Serving Boards of choice and to build a sincere and reliable relationship with each one of you. One experience at a time. When you buy one of our boards you are not just buying a Board you are purchasing a piece of History. We hope you will visit our site frequently for new products and special offers!
